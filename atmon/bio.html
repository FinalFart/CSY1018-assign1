About Jamie

They know me for my bubbly and easygoing personality, and I like to bring my friendliness to any groupwork I may be involved in.
I find I can diffuse any tention in the group with a whitty remark or pun.

When I'm not competing, however, I love playing video games, or coming up with wacky and interesting ideas for new ones.


One of my best skills is my problem solving. I love a challenge and embrace any opportunities I can get to show off new skills I have learned from solving various problems I have come across.
